# Code of Conduct

Contributors and maintainers must make participation harassment-free regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

Be direct about technical problems without attacking people. Accept correction, give actionable feedback, respect privacy, and do not publish private security reports or personal information. Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized conduct, and sustained disruption are not tolerated.

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and ban participants when behavior harms the project or its community. Report conduct problems privately through the maintainer contact or private security-advisory channel associated with the repository. Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential as far as practical.
